About Nike

Discover the rich history and mission of Nike.

Our History

Nike was founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight as Blue Ribbon Sports. It officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971. Since then, we have been a global leader in athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment.

Our Mission

At Nike, our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world. (*If you have a body, you are an athlete.) We strive to create groundbreaking sports innovations, promote sustainability, and empower athletes to reach their full potential.

Core Values

  • Innovation
  • Athlete Empowerment
  • Sustainability
  • Authenticity
  • Community Impact
